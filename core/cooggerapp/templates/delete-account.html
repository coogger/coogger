Deleting your user account

You can delete your Coogger user account at any time.

Deleting your user account removes your first, last and email addesss and all topics, bookmarks, votes, views, comments owned by your account. Issues and contributions you've made in topics owned by other users will not be deleted - instead, they'll be associated with our @Ghost user.


If you delete your account, you will never be able to log in to the coogger again.
